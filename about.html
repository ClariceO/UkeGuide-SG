About

Uke Guide is an online based platform own by Clarice Ong Si Jie.
Uke Guide aims to:
  • Help rookies learn how to play the ukulele
  • Provide a space for small businesses that sell ukuleles
  • Help our new musicians get to know more about the different types of ukueles,
  • and find out which type suits them best
On this website ukulele enthusiasts will be able purchase their very own ukuleles and master the basic knowledge of how to play it.

Meet Clarice

Clarice's Story

Clarice loved singing since she was a tad old. She was a pianist who likes to play songs on the ukulele before she tries it on the piano. This was because, to her it was easier to search up chords and play it on the uke. While using different ukulele websites she realized that they all have a rather complicated user interface (UI) which demotivated her in learning. Now that Clarice have played the ukulele for years she wants new players like her old self to learn all about the ukulele, just by using a single website. Uke Guide.
